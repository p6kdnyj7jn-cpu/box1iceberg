The Purge
The Purge was the major seperation of Box 1 members that occured from July 6th, 2023 to September 20th, 2023. The seperation stemmed from rising tension between members though at face was cutting contact with Crig as well as with anyone who didn't cut contact. This resulted in Dominick, Leila, Zana, Javi, Mari, Alex, Aidan, and Lucas–which were know as the Outer Circle–splitting from Crig, Michael, and Crib. Though Alex was a member of the Outer Circle, he secretly decided to stay neutral and kept contact with the three. The collapse of the Outer Circle came with a similar "purging" of Dominick, which would be reffered to as "The Englightenment".
